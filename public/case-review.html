White Card · Case review

Start your White Card case review.

If you worked at the Nevada Test Site, a DOE site, or an atomic-weapons plant — in any job — the White Card can pay for skilled home health for life, at no cost to you. Answer a few questions and a nurse reviews whether it can be opened for you. No claim forms, and nothing here is a determination of eligibility.

Alara helps you understand and reach benefits you may already be entitled to. A case review is a free clinical and eligibility read, not a coverage or eligibility determination. The Department of Labor decides EEOICPA claims. It does not replace your Resource Center, physician, or any federal agency.

Who we are

A nurse-owned home health agency that takes the coordination off your family.

Alara brings skilled nursing and therapy into the home across Clark County, Nevada, and coordinates all of it, the clinical care and the benefits paperwork, so nothing falls through and covered care carries no cost to you.

Why we exist

The care had moved into the home. The help had not caught up.

More care happens at home now than ever before: skilled nursing, wound care, infusion, therapy, the kind of treatment that used to require a hospital bed. But the families living it were left to run all of it themselves. They were coordinating nurses, chasing authorizations, and decoding benefits they had earned but were not receiving.

Alara was built to take that weight off the family. One team holds the clinical care and the paperwork together, so the people we serve can spend their attention on the person they love, not on a benefits program they should never have had to learn.

Who owns your care

Who owns the agency shows up in your care.

Two of the largest White Card home-health companies are owned by private-equity firms. Another is family-owned, but run by marketers, not clinicians. In each case a layer sits between whoever owns the business and whoever is accountable for your visit.

At Alara there is no layer. The owner is a nurse. The person responsible for the agency is the same kind of person responsible for your care, and a Director of Nursing reviews every start of care. We are independent, local to Las Vegas, and we intend to stay both.

And we do not stop at helping you file. Alara is the licensed agency that delivers the care your White Card covers, so the people who help you claim the benefit are the people who show up to provide it.

How we work

Four commitments built into how we run.

Not standard in home health. They hold on every admission and every visit.

A Director of Nursing reviews every start of care.

Our DON reviews every start-of-care assessment before it is submitted. Not a coordinator, not a supervisor. The most clinically accountable person in the agency reviews every admission.

We screen the whole person.

At every admission we screen for depression, malnutrition, social isolation, and caregiver burden using validated tools, because those things determine whether clinical treatment works. For White Card patients, we screen for consequential conditions that may qualify for expanded coverage at no additional cost.

One-hour response.

When you call Alara, you reach our clinical team within one hour during business hours. After hours, a nurse calls you first thing the next business day.

Zero authorization gaps.

Our scheduling blocks visits without a valid authorization number, and renewals are tracked in advance. Patients never receive care they are not covered for, and coverage does not lapse mid-episode. We coordinate billing across multiple payers so families never have to.

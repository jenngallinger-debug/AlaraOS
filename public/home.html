What being a patient looks like

You make one call. We handle everything else.

Around a federal-program patient sits a tangle of institutions that do not talk to each other. No person should have to connect them alone. Here is what we hold, from the first call onward.

EligibilityWe confirm everything you qualify for across every program you hold, including the benefits you were never told about.
ApprovalsWe assemble the physician orders and the documentation each program requires, and we submit them.
CoordinationYour physician, the DOL Resource Center, the VA and TriWest, your Authorized Representative. We are the messenger between them, so you are not.
BillingAcross every payer, so coverage never lapses and no cost lands on you.
VigilanceEvery visit, we watch for what is changing, and we act before it becomes a crisis. You are no longer the first to notice when something is wrong.

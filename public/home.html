What you may already be owed

A few of the benefits families come to us never knowing.

Each one is real, and each applies to specific situations. Most families are told a fraction of it. Identifying what applies to you and getting it authorized is the work we specialize in.

01
Most families are never told

You can be paid to care for your own mother or father.

Under the White Card’s in-home care benefit, the family member who already provides the daily care can be hired and paid for it as an employee, with a registered nurse’s supervision.

White Card · EEOICPA See how this works →

02
Hardly anyone is told this

A new problem your illness caused can be covered too.

When an accepted condition leads to something else, a wound that will not heal, an infection, a decline, that consequential condition can be covered once the Department of Labor accepts it. We handle the documentation that gets it approved.

White Card · EEOICPA See how this works →

03
You do not have to choose

Your White Card and your Medicare work together.

Having one does not cancel the other. For care tied to your accepted condition, the White Card generally pays, with nothing out of your pocket, and we coordinate the billing across both so you never have to.

White Card + Medicare See how this works →

04
For veterans

You can get skilled care at home without waiting on the VA.

Through VA Community Care, an eligible veteran receives home health from an approved community provider, often sooner than waiting for a VA appointment. You do not need Medicare to use it.

VA Community Care See how this works →

05
For federal and postal workers

An accepted on-the-job injury can cover care at home.

If OWCP has accepted your injury and a physician orders home health, it can be authorized at no out-of-pocket cost. Postal workers are covered the same as other federal employees.

OWCP · FECA See how this works →

Our founder

Alara was founded by a healthcare executive, clinician, and entrepreneur who has spent more than two decades building and scaling healthcare systems across the country.

She has worked alongside providers, health plans, regulators, and policymakers; helped shape programs serving vulnerable populations; built healthcare networks at scale; and led operations that have reached more than one million patients. After two decades improving healthcare at scale, she saw the center of care moving from institutions to the home, and founded Alara to build for that future.

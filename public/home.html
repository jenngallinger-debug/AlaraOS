What we do

Skilled care at home, and the coordination that makes it work.

A registered nurse holds the clinical care and the paperwork together, so nothing falls between them.

01

Skilled nursing

Registered and licensed nurses care for medically complex patients at home: assessment, wound care, medication management, IV and infusion therapy, and disease management for conditions like COPD, CHF, diabetes, cancer, and stroke. The kind of close watching that catches a problem before it becomes a hospital stay.
02

Wound care

A nurse treats chronic and non-healing wounds at home, including staging, sterile dressing changes, infection monitoring, and advanced or negative-pressure therapy when a physician orders it.
03

Physical and occupational therapy

Therapists rebuild strength, balance, and daily independence in the home where function has to happen: gait training, fall prevention, ADL retraining, adaptive equipment, and caregiver training, built around the goal the patient names.
04

Coordination and case management

We identify uncovered consequential conditions, track authorizations and renewals in advance, and coordinate billing across payers, so care is never interrupted by a paperwork gap.

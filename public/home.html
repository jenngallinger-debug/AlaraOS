Meet AlaraOS

Most agencies know one program. We built the system that knows all of them.

AlaraOS holds every federal benefit program, every covered service, and every authorization path in one place, and knows how they work together. The answer you get is the right one, with the federal source behind it.

White Card · EEOICPA Skilled care for accepted conditions, the consequential-condition pathway, and in-home benefits a family can be employed under.
OWCP · FECA Home health for federal and postal workers with an accepted on-the-job injury, including the authorization path most agencies cannot navigate.
VA Community Care Skilled care for eligible veterans through TriWest, Region 4, without waiting on a VA appointment.
Black Lung · DCMWC Covered medical care at home for coal miners with pneumoconiosis, and for their eligible survivors.
Medicare Skilled home health for homebound patients, coordinated alongside whatever federal benefit a patient also holds.
Across all of them Eligibility, what is covered, consequential conditions, and billing across every payer, so nothing a patient is entitled to is left on the table.

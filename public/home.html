Meet AlaraOS

The expertise, the instant you ask for it.

AlaraOS is the system behind Alara. Tell it your situation and it returns which federal benefits apply to you, what each one covers at home, the entitlements no one mentioned, and the exact path to get them approved. Two decades of federal-benefits expertise, the moment you need it, starting with the program we know more deeply than anyone: the White Card.

White Card · EEOICPA Our specialty, and the deepest in Nevada: skilled care for accepted conditions, the path to add new conditions the illness later causes, and the in-home benefit a spouse or child can be paid under.
OWCP · FECA Home health for federal and postal workers with an accepted on-the-job injury, including the authorization path most agencies get wrong.
VA Community Care Skilled care for eligible veterans through TriWest, Region 4, without waiting on a VA appointment.
Across all of them Eligibility, what is covered, the conditions you can add later, and billing across every payer, so nothing you are entitled to is left behind.

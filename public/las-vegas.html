Nevada’s own, and the benefits they earned here.

Las Vegas is home to the workers who built and ran the Nevada Test Site, now the Nevada National Security Site, along with federal and postal employees and veterans across the valley. Each of those groups has a federal benefit that can bring skilled care home. We work with all of them.

EEOICPA White Card · Test Site & DOE workers Compensation and free home health for life for Test Site and DOE workers, in any role, who got sick from the work. Local help, not a national call center.

The biggest names in White Card home health are run from Colorado and Utah, and two of them are owned by private-equity firms. Alara is nurse-owned and based here. When you call, you reach a local team, and a Director of Nursing reviews every start of care. We are independent, we are of this valley, and we intend to stay both.

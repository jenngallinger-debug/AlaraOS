Privacy

What this website knows about you, and what it never asks.

The tools on this site work without an account, without cookies that follow you, and without ads. This page says exactly what happens to anything you type here.

What you send us when you use a form

The case review, referral, and call-back tools send only what you type: a name, a phone number, and whatever you choose to add. Each submission goes to our nurse team so we can call you back — it is delivered to our care coordination inbox and kept in a submission record on our server so nothing is lost. It is used to respond to you and for nothing else.

  • Nothing is stored in your browser. No account is created and there is nothing for you to log into or delete.
  • Nothing is sold or shared for marketing. Submissions go to Alara’s clinical team only.
  • Please keep clinical records out of the forms. When care begins, medical records are handled through secure clinical channels, not this website.

Once you become a patient, your health information is protected under HIPAA and handled in our clinical systems. This website is the front door, not the medical record.

The estimators and the Navigator

The benefit Navigator, the impairment estimator, and the other calculators run entirely on your device. What you enter into them is not transmitted, recorded, or seen by anyone at Alara. Close the page and it is gone.

Anonymous usage signals

To learn which questions matter most to families, the site records anonymous events — for example, “a visitor opened the care-hours guide” or “someone started a case review.” These events contain no name, no phone number, no email, and no IP address, and they are not tied to you across visits. There is no Google Analytics, no Facebook pixel, and no advertising tracker on this site.

Calling or emailing us

If you call (702) 814-9630 or email referrals@alarahomecare.com, that conversation lives in our phone and email systems like any call or message to a clinic, and is used to coordinate your care.

Questions

Ask us directly. Call (702) 814-9630 or write to referrals@alarahomecare.com and a member of our team will answer.

Inside AlaraOS

The operating system behind your care.

Every Alara nurse arrives backed by AlaraOS, the intelligence that runs the work around your care. It holds your benefits, your providers, and your treatment in one place, works out what needs to happen, and follows through, so you reach one organization that already understands you.

Software, and something more

An EMR remembers. An operating system runs things.

Most of healthcare runs on software that records what happened: your visits, your notes, your bills. It is useful, and it only ever looks backward.

AlaraOS is different in kind. It holds the full picture of your care at once, and then it acts on it, working out every benefit you qualify for, lining up the people involved, and making sure the care actually reaches you. A record looks back at what was done. AlaraOS moves your care forward.

How it works

One continuous loop, running quietly behind every visit.

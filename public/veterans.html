VA home health care for veterans in Las Vegas.

Alara is enrolling as a VA Community Care Network provider for Region 4. If you are a veteran in Southern Nevada who needs skilled care at home, here is how it works and how to start.

See if you qualify → Talk to a nurse

What the VA covers at home.

When the VA authorizes home health through Community Care, your covered skilled care comes at no cost to you. For an authorized plan of care, that can include:

  • Skilled nursing: assessment, wound care, medication management, and disease management at home.
  • Physical and occupational therapy: strength, mobility, balance, and returning to daily activities safely.
  • Home health aide services: personal care under nursing supervision.
  • Medical social work: connecting you to resources and coordinating with your VA care team.

For VA providers: how to refer to Alara.

Referring to Alara is straightforward, and we move fast. Call us at (702) 814-9630, or submit through your standard VA Community Care process.

Once we receive the referral

  1. We contact the veteran within 4 business hours.
  2. We verify the authorization.
  3. We schedule start of care, typically within 48 to 72 hours.

What we need

  • Veteran name and contact information
  • VA authorization number
  • Physician order (services and frequency)
  • Medication list, if available

We handle everything else, including coordination, scheduling, and follow-up. For complex cases, our clinical director is available to consult before referral.

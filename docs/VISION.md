Alara is the operating system for care that sits above the EMR — holding the full
reality of the person, the family, the providers, the benefits, the workflows, and
the promises, so every stakeholder experiences one intelligent organization instead
of a fragmented system.

## What this governs

This is the **top-level expression of Alara**. Everything derives from it, in this order:

1. **The Vision** (this statement) — what Alara *is*.
2. **The Operating Cycle** — *how* Alara achieves it:
   Perceive → Understand → Judge → Orchestrate → Act → Communicate → Verify → Learn.
3. **The Experience Moments** (Human Experience layer) — *how a person feels* it
   (The Answer, Being Understood, The First Kept Promise, …).
4. **The Interfaces** — website, voice, portal, SMS, Care Guide, nurse, EMR — each a
   surface where the vision becomes real for a stakeholder.

## What it means for the website

The website is one interface (a Public Experience Layer surface). Its job is to make
a person feel they have reached **one intelligent organization, not a fragmented
system** — and to remove enough uncertainty that the right person chooses to begin.
It is derived through the Experience Moments; it is never built straight from
architecture to pixels.

# AlaraOS — Capabilities (Engage · Deliver · Sustain · Assure)

Definitions authoritative **as written**.

## Engage
- **Purpose:** bring relationships, opportunities, people, and organizations to
  deliberate disposition.
- **Invariant:** disposition. Engage ends when relationship uncertainty ends.
- **Disposition may be:** accepted, declined, redirected, referred, waitlisted,
  nurtured, deferred, or partnered.

## Deliver
- **Purpose:** fulfill obligations the organization is bound to honor.
- **Invariant:** obligation. An obligation is an obligation-state borne by a
  Relationship, arising from a source, and discharged through Deliver.
- **Sources include:** promise, law, regulation, care relationship, professional duty,
  employment, contract, Constitution, and implied acceptance.

## Sustain
- **Purpose:** preserve organizational continuity.
- **Invariant:** continuity. Continuity is the non-dischargeable precondition that
  allows the organization to keep operating and honoring obligations.

## Assure
- **Purpose:** verify reality matches its intended standard and correct the gap.
- **Invariant:** intended standard. Standard resolves to **Knowledge** — knowledge of
  what should be. **There is no Standard object.**

## Cross-cutting rules
- Every capability runs the **complete** Operating Cycle.
- Capabilities own **cycle instances**, not Objects.
- **Objects own state.**
- Shared Object writes **serialize through the Object state machine**.
